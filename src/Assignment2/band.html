Albums

Il ballo della vita
Il ballo della vita is Maneskins debut albuum. It is a relatively short album, featuring only 34 minutes of songs.
It also is prominently in italian (as this band is italian). Despite the short length and language barrier,
this album is still a great listen, with many amazing songs to listen to.
4/5
Teatro d'ira: Vol. I
Teatro d'ira is a shorter album, but still has many great songs that make this album. However,
most of the songs are in italian, so those unfamiliar with the language, or who prefer being able
to understand the lyrics, might dislike this album. In total, it has about 29 minutes of songs.
4/5
Rush!
Rush is the best album created by Maneskin, and features great variance in the types of music.
There is plenty of songs that will appeal to a wide variety of listeners. It is a good length album,
featuring around 67 minutes of songs when listening to the bonus tracks as well.
5/5
